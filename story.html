Our Story

Our journey as BSIS E2025 began on the first day of classes, August 11, 2025. At the start, we were a group of students cautiously stepping into a new chapter of our lives. The first days were filled with shy introductions, tentative smiles, and the excitement of meeting new blockmates. Slowly, we began to form our own circle of friends within the block section. Some revealed their intelligence, others their humor, creating a lively vibe that made our days unforgettable. As time passed, our bonds grew stronger. We discovered each other’s strengths, shared laughter during study sessions, and supported one another in both academics and personal growth. Even our professors, initially intimidating figures, became memorable parts of our journey as we built positive relationships and learned under their guidance. Together, we celebrated small victories, overcame challenges, and created countless memories that will last a lifetime. Our story is a testament to friendship, teamwork, and the joy of learning in a supportive and fun environment. Every day added new experiences, and our block section blossomed into a thriving, united community. This website captures the spirit, energy, and unforgettable moments of BSIS E2025, reflecting how far we’ve come and all that we’ve accomplished together.
